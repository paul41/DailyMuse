Watch | Minor Hotels sees India as key growth market, plans 50 hotels in 10 years

Minor Hotels is aiming to expand aggressively in India, targeting 50 properties over the next decade. Speaking to CNBC-TV18, William Heinecke, Chairman of Minor Hotels, said, “The challenge is to reach 50 hotels within 10 years.”Heinecke acknowledged that the company has been a late entrant in India, noting, “We have been a late starter in India because we only just started our journey 25 years ago.” With a presence in multiple countries around the world, Minor Hotels now sees India as a key focus for growth.The company has also expanded its portfolio with several new brands, reflecting its strategy to tap into the country’s booming hospitality market. Heinecke added, “India is a very opportune market for us right now,” signalling confidence in the long-term potential of the Indian hotel sector.
