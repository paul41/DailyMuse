Gas supply cuts force Morbi Ceramic units to halt production: Industry body

Ceramic manufacturers in Morbi have begun shutting factories after gas supplies were cut sharply, according to Morbi Ceramic Association President Manoj Aervadiya. Gujarat Gas has reduced supply by about 50%, while propane and LNG deliveries have largely stopped, creating a severe fuel shortage for the industry. The sector, which has an annual turnover of around ₹70,000 crore, depends heavily on gas for production. Industry players warn that prolonged disruptions and higher gas prices could halt exports and force companies to raise product prices or suspend operations.
